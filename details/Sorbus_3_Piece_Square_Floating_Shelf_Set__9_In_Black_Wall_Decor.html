Upgrade Your Home Decor with the Sorbus 3-Piece Floating Shelves

The Sorbus 3-piece square floating shelves offer a perfect blend of elegance and utility. Made from durable faux wood with a sleek matte black finish, these shelves are designed to enhance the style of any interior space while providing functional storage. Their minimalist aesthetic makes them ideal for modern or traditional decors, adding a touch of sophistication to your living room, bedroom, or bathroom.

Each shelf measures 9.25" x 9.25" and is 1.5" thick, giving you ample room to showcase treasured items like family photos, trophies, plants, or decorative accents. The invisible mounting brackets ensure a clean, seamless look, allowing the shelves to float effortlessly on the wall and disappear into the background, adding to the overall visual appeal.

Whether you're looking to free up floor space, elevate your decor, or add a functional element to your room, these floating shelves are a great choice. They're easy to install with all necessary hardware included, and their versatile design allows them to adapt to various settings, from rustic to farmhouse and beyond.

These shelves can support up to 8 pounds per unit, making them suitable for holding lightweight items securely. The simplicity of their design makes them a perfect gift for any occasion, including Christmas, Halloween, and New Year's. Add a touch of class to your home with the Sorbus floating shelves.
