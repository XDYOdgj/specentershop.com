A Beautifully Crafted Purple Cremation Urn

This elegant purple cremation vessel is a meaningful tribute designed to honor the life and legacy of a loved one. Crafted from premium aluminum, it boasts a radiant high-gloss lacquer finish and a refined silver rim detail that adds a touch of sophistication to any display.

This urn is part of the Splendore Series, known for its artistry and timeless appeal. With a 200 cubic inch capacity, it is perfectly sized to hold adult cremains while still allowing for a complete and striking memorial presentation. Whether placed in a private home, a memorial chapel, or a columbarium niche, this urn enhances the ambiance with its graceful presence.

Key Features

  • Premium aluminum construction with a high-gloss lacquer finish
  • 200 cubic inch capacity ideal for adult cremains
  • Fine silver or stainless steel engraved design
  • Secure 4.7" threaded lid with a 3.6" opening
  • Felt-lined base to protect surfaces and enhance display aesthetics

Why Choose This Urn?

Whether you're looking for a custom cremation urn to cherish and display, or a personalized memorial urn that reflects your unique appreciation for your loved one, this urn offers the perfect blend of durability, beauty, and sentimental value. Its secure lid ensures safe storage, while the moisture-resistant coating helps protect the contents over time. The felt-lined base not only prevents scratches but also adds an extra layer of elegance to your memorial setup.

Designed for both practicality and emotional resonance, this urn is a meaningful way to honor your loved one with dignity, grace, and artistic beauty. The Splendore Series is a testament to the craftsmanship and care that goes into every piece, making it a standout choice for any memorial space.
