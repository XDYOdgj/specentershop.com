Mr. & Mrs. Wooden Sign

This beautiful Mr. & Mrs. sign brings a warm, homely vibe to your space with its rustic home accent design. The soft, faded white wood creates a vintage feel that's both charming and timeless, making it a versatile addition to your wooden wedding decor or seasonal wreath themes. Whether you're looking to decorate your decorative wall art or add a personal touch to your wedding table, this sign is a perfect choice.

Measuring 15" x 5", it's a striking piece that stands out in any room. The durable 3/16" thick MDF board features a printed paper text, ensuring long-lasting use indoors and outdoors. It comes with a sturdy jute rope for easy hanging and pairs well with other rustic home accent elements such as wreaths, garlands, and themed holiday items like holly, mistletoe, shamrocks, and snowmen. This is a thoughtful gift for newlyweds or a stylish way to bring personality to your living space all year round.
